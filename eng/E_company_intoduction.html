About Us

We are continually challenging ourselves to become a leading company
specialized in in-vitro diagnostics and dedicated to creating value
for human health innovation.

We are the pioneers of diagnostic kit and technology, specializing in the area of in-vitro diagnostics with excellent source of technology.
Rapigen's products, which were the first to be commercialized using the Black Gold Nano Particles technology within the rapid diagnostic kit industry, are manufactured based on excellent standards of product quality and improved reproducibility.
Currently, we are exporting our products to more than 52 countries around the world, carrying out various projects and collaborations with diverse international entities, and pioneering a comprehensive array of services, to support diversified OEM customization service through an innovative R&D.
