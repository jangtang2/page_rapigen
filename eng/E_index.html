Rapigen has been designated a promising company for overseas procurement markets in the first quarter of 2024 by the Public Procurement Service
'G-PASS (Government Performance Assured)' is a system implemented by the Public Procurement Service's Overseas Procurement Information Center to target overseas government procurement markets strategically. It selects promising small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) with a high potential for entering overseas procurement markets. In particular, it provides various support measures to companies with export competitiveness for government procurement in target countries, utilizing the Public Procurement Service's overseas network. Being selected as a G-PASS company recognizes the export competitiveness in overseas markets, technical expertise, and reliability. Moreover, attaching the 'G-PASS Company Mark' to the registration of a comprehensive shopping mall in the national marketplace proves the company's technical expertise and reliability certified by the government. Additionally, designated benefits include support for participation in overseas exhibitions and consultation meetings with overseas buyers. Park Jae-gu, CEO of Rapigen, commented, 'The selection of Rapigen as a promising company for overseas procurement markets is a great example of our company's recognized potential and prospects for overseas procurement fulfillment.' He added, 'As overseas procurement markets are significant and attractive, we will utilize the G-PASS certification to create new trends in rapid and accurate diagnosis of various diseases, including medical conditions in the K-medical sector.'
